BookWorm Details

BookWorm is an app for organizing your book collection. Future plans are to add the ability to sync with multiple online book sources (import/export) and include book reviews and social features (in the works).

Please let me know if you come across any new issues, the project site issue tracker is where currently known issues and enhancement plans are logged. Thanks!
